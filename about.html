About Me

My name is Iris Currie. I am a third year Games + Simulations major focusing on Game development and programming, with a minor in Computer Science. I have grown up playing video games, and it has been a long time dream of mine to make them, but for many years that's all it was, a dream, as I spent most of my time focusing on theater and acting. But now that I have changed my focuses I am striving to make that old dream a reality, taking as many classes as I can to learn every facet of the game design industry, meanwhile taking what time I can to slowly work on a few personal projects of mine.


I additionally spend my time at college participating in a few extracurriculars. Those being the Miami University Game Design Club, as well as being a member of the Miami University Varsity Street Fighter 6 team.
