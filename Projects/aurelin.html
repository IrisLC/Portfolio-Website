World Design

Final deliverable for a Writing for Games/World building class. Created a Dungeons and Dragons campaign setting over the course of a semester and eventually compressed some of the world into a document in the format of a common campaign setting guide. This guide includes maps of the area, history of the world, points of interest, and tips for Dungeon Masters to create their own cities/dungeons that are uniquely flavored to the world

Additionally, have been running a personal campaign using this setting for a group of 6 players for a few months now.
