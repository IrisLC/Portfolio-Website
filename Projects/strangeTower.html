Strange Tower

Unity, Enemy Programmer, Fall 2024

A first person roguelite/beat'emup project developed over the course of a the Fall 2024 semester for a class in Indie Game Development. Worked as a game designer and as one of two programmers in a team of 5, focusing on programming the enemy behaviors as well as debugging interactions between the player and enemies. Developed in Unity 2023, and programmed in C#.

This project served as largely a learning experience, as this was my first experience working with:

  1. Unity's Navmesh
  2. Setting up/using Github in a team
  3. developing a game from concept to finished product
